# Long Long Ago

**Provenance:** Thomas Haynes Bayly, 1833. Public domain.

**Range:** G3–E5. Opens on the G string (open G) — one of only two beginner
tunes that starts on the lowest string. The E5 in bar 11 is on the first finger
of the E string.

**Practice tips:**
- The 3/4 waltz feel: down-bow on beat 1 (strong), up-bow on beats 2–3 (lighter).
- The first note D (open D string) leads up to G — think of it as a pickup breath.
- Bar 11 peak (E5): cross from A string to E string. Practice the string crossing
  in slow motion before adding it to the full tune.
- This tune is also known as "the lullaby tune" — keep the tone warm and
  connected, never harsh.
