# Meadow Run

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this acoustic violin
build. MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's acoustic violin (GDAE standard tuning, first position).

**Range:** G3–G5. The G5 (top of the B section) is the 3rd finger on the E string.

**Form:** AABB. Each section eight bars.

**Character:** Brisk American fiddle-style reel, but written with bowing in mind
rather than pure dance-floor drive.

**Practice tips:**
- The A section stays on D and A strings mostly — a smooth workout.
- The B section's peak (g4 = G5 in bar 14) requires crossing to the E string;
  approach with a up-bow so the crossing falls naturally.
- At ♩=108 this should feel like a comfortable jog — purposeful but not breathless.
- Try it slurred (two notes per bow) once you know the notes: G–A slurred, B–D
  slurred, etc. It changes the character completely.
