# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished acoustic violin standing as a real physical object: the classic hourglass body silhouette with gently curved upper and lower bouts and a waisted center, a spruce top with fine straight grain and a warm maple back with subtle figure, both plates carried over a graceful arch. Carved scroll volute crowning a slender maple neck, ebony fingerboard sweeping down to a fitted bridge with two paired f-holes flanking the bridge line on the top plate. Four strings run from a scrolled pegbox over the nut, across the fingerboard and bridge, to a tailpiece anchored at the lower end, with a chinrest at the edge near the tailpiece. Hand-rubbed amber-to-golden-brown varnish over the wood, glossy but not plastic, with purfling tracing the plate edges. Realistic scale of a four-string violin held against a player, clean neutral light-gray studio background, soft directional studio light raking across the arch to reveal grain and varnish depth, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

An acoustic violin staged on a luthier's workbench in a warm wood-toned workshop, resting on a soft cloth beside hand tools and a coil of spare strings, late-afternoon natural light through a side window catching the amber varnish and the arch of the spruce top. Wood shavings and a straightedge nearby hint at neck-projection and setup work in progress. Shallow depth of field with the scroll and f-holes in sharp focus and the background softly blurred, environmental storytelling, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the violin top centered on the bridge and the two f-holes: the maple bridge standing on its two feet with the four strings crossing its arched top, the carved f-holes flanking it, and the fine purfling line near the plate edge. Raking low light skims the spruce grain and the curvature of the arching, revealing the satin sheen of the varnish and the crisp carved edges of the f-holes. High detail, sharp focus on wood texture and string-to-bridge contact, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no impossible mechanisms or extra strings/pegs; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
