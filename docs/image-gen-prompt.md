# Image Generation Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, setup measurements, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of a finished four-string acoustic violin reference build: traditional violin silhouette with spruce top, maple back and ribs, ebony fingerboard, carved bridge, tailpiece with fine tuners, four pegs, f-holes, and a warm transparent amber-brown varnish that shows wood grain. Keep the instrument realistic and luthier-made, with no exaggerated arching or fantasy decoration. Show the bridge line, strings G-D-A-E, soundpost/bridge setup implied but not cut away. Clean studio background, soft directional studio lighting, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic luthier workshop scene of the acoustic violin on a padded bench during setup review, with a fitted bridge, soundpost setter, peg shaver, strings, and a small tuner nearby. A luthier's hands gently support the instrument while checking bridge alignment under low tension. Natural window light, wood shavings and hand tools in the background, realistic setup process, no dimension marks or final fabrication claims.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the violin bridge and f-hole area: carved maple bridge feet fitted to the spruce top, four strings crossing the bridge, subtle varnish texture, f-hole edge, and a hint of the soundpost position through the f-hole without making it a technical drawing. Shallow depth of field, soft side light, high detail, believable luthiery surfaces.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, labels, watermarks, brand marks, extra limbs, impossible string count, distorted bridge, fantasy inlays, cutaway dimension callouts, DXF-like line art, or setup measurements; hands must be anatomically plausible if shown.
